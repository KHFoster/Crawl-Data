Transportation could be quite pricy if you do not make a smart decision. With the demand for cheap transportation rising, more and more people are moving on to purchasing pre-owned cars. The good news is that you need not spend a fortune to <a href="http://www.tell-n-sell.com/s-Domestic-Cars-Classifieds-33-3.aspx">buy great domestic cars</a>. There are so many alternatives with the added option of buying either new or used cars for sale that by the time you create a short list, you would have spent a lot of time and exhausted most of your energy! Car hunting could be a tiresome job altogether.</p> <p dir="ltr">Not anymore! You can do all the research and short listing within a few clicks. Tell-n-Sell has made your car hunt a lot easier. We offer you a myriad of options for Classic Cars, Domestic Cars, SUVs and VANs to select from.</p> <p dir="ltr">If you are looking for <a href="http://www.tell-n-sell.com/used-cars-under-10000-savannah.aspx">buying used cars under $10000</a>, here are a few things you need to consider:</p> <p dir="ltr"> </p><p dir="ltr"><strong>Buy as new as you can afford</strong></p> <p dir="ltr">Newer cars have all the latest features that the manufacturer might have introduced. These include safety and security features, some bug fixes from the previous models, and also interesting new features to make your drive smooth and fun! So, even if you are looking at pre-owned cars, make sure that you buy as new as you can afford.</p> <p dir="ltr">If the car is too old, it will definitely have a lot of maintenance costs attached to it. You would rather spend one time than incur a recurring maintenance of a car that was simply old and cheap!</p> <p dir="ltr"> </p><p dir="ltr"><strong>Check out the seller</strong></p> <p dir="ltr">Even if you are buying online, you need to check out the seller. Whether the ad was placed by the owner himself or by an agency, you must do a little research yourself. Go online and check whether the seller has a website or any social media page. Also reading reviews about the seller proves to be helpful.</p> <p dir="ltr"> </p><p dir="ltr"><strong>Check out the vehicle history</strong></p> <p dir="ltr">It is important to check the history of a used car. Especially if the owner himself is not the one selling it, you will need to thoroughly check out why the car has been put up for sale. Ask for the owner’s name and vehicle purchase details. Also, ask for the previous owner’s contact number. Ensure that you learn about all the previous work done – like replacement of parts, repairs, and so on. Only the original owner will be able to give you all the detailed information.</p> <p dir="ltr">Overall, if you are looking for pre-owned cars or <a href="http://www.tell-n-sell.com/s-Classic-Cars-Classifieds-33-2.aspx">used cars</a>, it is best that you look for fairly new ones. If your budget is not too tight, look for those that have had the least amount of maintenance work done. Well, you’ll need to do a lot of digging, but you’ll surely find the model that you feel is a ‘must have’!</p> <p dir="ltr">Patience and persistence is the key!
